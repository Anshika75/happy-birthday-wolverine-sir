Happy Birthday

Instead of counting candles, Or tallying the years, Contemplate your blessings now, As your birthday nears. Consider special people Who love you, and who care, And others who've enriched your life Just by being there. Think about the memories Passing years can never mar, Experiences great and small That have made you who you are. Another year is a happy gift, So cut your cake, and say, "Instead of counting birthdays, I count blessings every day!"
May you have a special day, May your heart be full of cheer, May love and laughter find you, May your smiles be ear to ear.  May your joy be everlasting, May only good things come your way, May all your prayers be answered, May you be blessed on your birthday
